Phoeinx School Management Solution

Phoenix is an in-house school management solution, developed leveraging our extensive knowledge in Education and School Operations. Based on feedback from our early adopters and the rapidly evolving education landscape, Phoenix has been developed to ensure your needs are met today and well into the future. Simple but diversified solution that adapts to multiple curricula, with user based access logins for multiple roles and responsibilities. Apart from catering to Academics, Phoenix also caters to other departmental functions like HR, Finance and school operations. It also provides the ability to manage transport service.
